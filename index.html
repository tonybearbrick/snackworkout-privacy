Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-22

About this app

Snack Workout (the "App") is a personal-use fitness reminder app. It does not require an account and stores no personal data on any server we operate.

Data we collect directly

None. All settings (workout type, reps, work hours, streak count) are stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage. We have no servers, no analytics, and no user accounts.

Third-party services

The App displays banner advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission when prompted by iOS.
  • Approximate location derived from IP address, device type, and other technical signals used by Google to serve ads.

Google's privacy practices are described at policies.google.com/privacy.

App Tracking Transparency

iOS will ask whether you allow the App to track you across other apps and websites. You can decline; the App will still function normally and will show non-personalized ads instead.

Notifications

The App schedules local notifications on your device to remind you to do a quick workout during your configured work hours. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device — nothing is sent to a server.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The effective date at the top will change accordingly.
